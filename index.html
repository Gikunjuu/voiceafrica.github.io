Changing the lives of children with disabilities

Find out what we do

There are 60 million people with a disability living in Africa, the vast majority of which are children.

Up to 80% of these children will not live to be 5 years old. The ones that do are 4 times more likely to be abused, 10 times more likely to be out of school.

WITH YOUR SUPPORT

It doesn’t have to be this way. We believe every child is able.
