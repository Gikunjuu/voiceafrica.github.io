Purity N Simiyu

Chief Executive Officer

Purity leads the team at HSVA on matters of planning, governance and resource mobilization. She is the Commercial and Country manager of Muenzer Kenya ltd a subsidiary of Munzer Bioindustries Austria. Board member at Aga khan primary school representing the deaf from Grade one to Eight, Machakos National school as Parent representative and International Christian Centre church Imara as pioneer of deaf ministry. She has studied Bachelor of commerce, Marketing and Master in Business strategic management at Kenyatta university.

Florence Nyaga

Head of Programmes

Deborah leads the team at HSVA on matters of partnerships and legal affairs. She is the Country manager of Giertsen Energy solutions Kenya, a subsidiary of Giertsen Norway. She holds L.L.B Honours, Law Degree Westminster university 1994, International relations, post graduate Diploma, University of Stockholm 2003. She sits in several board meetings including Swedish trade and investment council and other children welfare councils.
